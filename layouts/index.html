Meet the Author

Debashish Chakrabarty

Debashish is a software professional based in Pune, India, with decades of experience in Agile transformations across startups and enterprises. He has been blogging since 2002 in English and Hindi, sharing insights on software development, Agile practices, and technology leadership.

He founded India's first blog awards Indibloggies, pioneered Hindi blogging platforms like Chittha Vishwa, and created Podbharati, India's first Hindi podcast platform. His work has been recognized with nominations for prestigious awards including the TATA Nen Hottest Startup award.

Currently, he publishes newsletters The Agile Chronicles and Podcast Parikrama, sharing practical wisdom on Agile methodologies and the evolving audio landscape.
